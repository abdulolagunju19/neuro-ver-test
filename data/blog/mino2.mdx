#### Let's Get Back to It

What is the most interesting part of any journey? People nowadays like to say, “The path you take is what’s important, not the destination.” I don’t believe that. If I’m going on holiday to the Bahamas, I don’t really care if I get there by boat or by plane. I keep that same mindset when I think about scientific research, because I care more about how the burger tastes than how you make the burger. So, with that in mind, it’s time to learn about the results of the experiment.
